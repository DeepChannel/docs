Click **Terminal** or press <Keybinding mac="⇧ ⌘ `" winux="ctrl+shift+`" /> to open a new Terminal instance.

<img src="/product/terminal.png"></img>

### Terminal Management
Use the dropdown to jump between multiple instances, the **New Terminal** button to create a new instance, and **Delete Terminal** to delete the current instance.

<img src="/product/terminal-management.png"></img>
